Understanding Your Energy Consumption

Welcome! This interactive chart visualizes the energy consumption for your devices and locations over the last 24 hours. By default, it shows the total energy used by all your devices in various locations. The vertical bars represent the amount of energy used (measured in kilowatt-hours, kWh), while the horizontal labels show either the devices or the addresses of your locations. You can switch between viewing 'Devices' and 'Locations' to see how energy is used across individual devices or specific locations. This chart helps you compare and understand energy usage patterns, supporting informed decisions for efficient energy management.
